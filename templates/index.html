The SAT Maths section covers a total of 44 questions. Candidates have to complete this section in 70 minutes (1 hour 10 minutes). The 44 questions will be divided into two modules of 22 each. Module 1 will cover 20 operational questions and 2 pretext questions, where the time duration is 35 minutes. Module 2 is also similar to module 1, with 20 operational questions and 2 pretext questions. The scoring in this section will range from 200 to 800. A score of 700 is considered good, and 700 to 580 is average.
